Critical Gap — Expected Income Not Found

⚠ SAYCO / ACINPRO Performance Royalties — Completely Absent

SAYCO (Sociedad de Autores y Compositores de Colombia) is the mandatory collective management organization for musical authors and composers in Colombia. For any song performed publicly, broadcast on radio or TV, or played in commercial venues in Colombia, SAYCO is legally required to collect and distribute performance royalties. ACINPRO covers performers and producers.

Canticuentos songs have been broadcast on Colombian children's television, radio, and educational platforms since 1975. For a catalog of this size and cultural prominence to generate zero SAYCO income in an entire calendar year is exceptional — and demands immediate clarification.

Possibilities include: (1) SAYCO income is being collected but retained at the publisher level and not passed through; (2) SAYCO has not been correctly registered for this catalog; (3) Income is being reported under a different payee name; (4) The statements provided do not represent the complete picture of income streams. Additionally, SAYCO's own compliance issues (fined COP 5.3 billion in Nov 2025) may be a contributing factor requiring independent investigation.

Colombia imposes a 20–25% withholding tax on royalties to non-residents. These statements should also be checked for correct WHT application on all Colombia-territory payments.

Legal & Contextual Background

Publisher Structure

  • Spirit Latin (Prodemus) is the publishing administrator — Spirit Music Latino acquired Prodemus Colombia in 2019.
  • Sunflower Entertainment holds the JV rights to the Codiscos publishing catalog.
  • Codiscos owns the Canticuentos trademark and master recordings separately from composition rights.
  • These statements cover composition/publishing rights only.

Colombian Law

  • Economic rights: life + 80 years. Moral rights: perpetual and inalienable.
  • Transfers of rights are limited to specific modalities, territories, and durations stated in contract.
  • 20–25% withholding tax on royalties paid to non-residents. Verify WHT application across all Colombia payments.
  • SAYCO fined COP 5.3 billion (~$1.2M USD) in November 2025 for monopoly abuse and restrictive administrative practices.

Songwriter

  • Marlore Anwandter (also known as Marian Margarita Johnson) — Chilean-born, arrived Colombia 1973.
  • Wrote 80+ Canticuentos compositions beginning 1975.
  • PAJARO CARPINTERO EL has documented co-writers: Luis Gomez Ramirez and Mario Hernandez.
  • CRAZY WITCH THE has an unknown/unlisted co-writer per statement data.
